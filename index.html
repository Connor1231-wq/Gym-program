Phase 1 — Base build
Goal: 0 → 5+ pull-ups by race day
Follow this progression every gym session. Don't skip steps — most people fail because of weak scapular control, not weak arms.
Pull-up progression steps
Step 1 — Foundation (weeks 1–3)
1
Dead hangs
3 × 20–30 sec — builds grip and shoulder packing
Do every gym day this phase
3×30s
2
Scapular pulls
Hang on bar, retract shoulder blades without bending arms. 3 × 8 reps
This is the key movement most people skip
3×8
Step 2 — Build (weeks 3–8)
3
Band-assisted pull-ups
3 × 5–8 reps with thick band. Full range of motion. Progress to thinner band every 2–3 weeks.
3×5–8
4
Negative pull-ups
Jump or step to top position. Lower in 4–6 seconds. 3 × 3–5 reps.
Add from week 4 when scapular control is solid
3×5
Step 3 — First rep (weeks 6–10)
5
Attempt unassisted rep
Try 1 unassisted rep at the very start of each session when totally fresh. Don't force it — it'll come.
Most people hit their first rep weeks 6–8 with consistent training
1×1

🏁 Spartan Race — 19 September 2025
Sprint distance: ~5km, 20–30 obstacles. 30 burpee penalty for each failed obstacle. Train specifically for what you'll face.
Key obstacles to train for
🤜
Monkey bars / rig
Dead hangs, brachiation on bar, farmer carries for grip endurance. Target 45+ sec hang by race day.
🪣
Bucket / sandbag carry
Goblet squats, front rack carries, heavy KB holds. Train loaded carries every Friday session.
🧱
Wall climbs
Box jump-overs, push/pull combos, explosive hip drive. Practice the wall if your gym has one.
🏃
Trail running & elevation
Your Thu 5km + Sun long run. Add hill reps in phase 3. Spartan terrain is rarely flat.
💥
Burpee penalty
30 burpees per failed obstacle. Build to 30 in under 3 min. Add burpees to every Wed finisher.

🏁 RACE DAY
Trust the training. Attack every obstacle.
Race day tips
1
Attempt every obstacle — don't give up early and take the burpee penalty unnecessarily.
2
Run easy between obstacles — preserve energy for the obstacle itself.
3
Grip the bars firmly but not white-knuckle tight — you'll fatigue faster.
4
Hyrox training translates directly — the conditioning you've built is exactly what's needed.
